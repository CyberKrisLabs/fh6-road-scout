Place your FH6 fast-travel indicator template here as:
  ft_indicator.png

How to capture it:
1. Open Forza Horizon 6 and go to the World Map.
2. Hover over any road you have already explored.
3. Take a screenshot (Win+Shift+S or your preferred tool).
4. Crop out just the fast-travel hover tooltip/icon that appears.
5. Save the cropped image as ft_indicator.png in this folder.

The template should be ~100-200px wide and include the distinctive
fast-travel UI element (icon + label). A clean background-free crop
gives the best template match results.
